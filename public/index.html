Optimize your building's energy efficiency

About the Project

The Building Energy Analysis tool is designed to help architects, and building owners assess and improve the energy performance of their structures. By inputting key building parameters, users can compare their building's energy usage against established baselines and visualize potential energy savings.

Our goal is to promote sustainable building practices by providing actionable insights that lead to reduced energy consumption, lower operational costs, and a smaller environmental footprint.

Key Features

Data Input

Input detailed building parameters and characteristics for accurate energy calculations

Baseline Comparison

Compare your building's energy performance against industry-standard baselines.

Storage

Store and access your building analyses for future reference.

How It Works

  1. Building Parameters: Enter basic information about your building including climate, floor area, and type
  2. Building Characteristics: Specify details like insulation, windows, and shading
  3. View Results: The tool will calculate energy usage and compare it to baseline scenarios.
  4. Save & Compare: Store building configurations for future reference
